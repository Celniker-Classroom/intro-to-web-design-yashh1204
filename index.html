My Early Life

My Name is Yashmit Bhatmuley, and I am 16 years old. I was born in San Diego, California and have lived here my whole life. When I was 2, I moved from my home in North Park to my home in Carmel Valley. For elementary school, I attended Ashley Falls; for middle school, I attended Pacific Trails; and for high school, I am attending Canyon Crest Academy. I like doing many things, such as Mock Trial, Robotics, and playing badminton.

My Current Interests

I like doing all sorts of things. I've never had one thing that I've done my whole life. I've always been switching between different hobbies because I like trying new things. Some things I really enjoy doing are singing, playing badminton, playing the guitar, and hanging out with friends. Recently, I've been incorporating my singing into playing the guitar. I didn’t always like badminton; actually, I hated it at first because my mom would force me to go to classes. However, when I started playing with friends, I found it super fun and a good way to spend time with them.

People that Inspire Me

Somebody who really inspires me is my dad, but also my mom, for different reasons. My dad showed me that it’s never too late to chase your dreams. My dad, who was an engineer until he was 43, decided one day that engineering wasn't for him and that he wanted to do something different in life. Because of this he decided to go into law and found himself enjoying his job much more. Additionally, my mom showed me that you can do what you love and still make money. My mom has always loved singing, so she started teaching other kids to sing, turning her hobby into a job.
